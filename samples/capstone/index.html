Capstone Project

Published: Dec 18, 2025

Abstract

These are the slides and demonstration video for the final group capstone project of my degree. This project was completed collaboratively with Brock Byard and Jacob Derenzy. The goal was to simulate the environment of a security operations center by detecting suspicious network activity (generated by us) related to our assets, and then perform an investigation on the captured information using the tools that we deployed.
